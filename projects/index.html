Projects

A glimpse into my career journey reveals a diverse spectrum of projects I've had the privilege to engage with.

These undertakings span various industries and creative challenges:

    • 2021

  2. Place

    Valentino

    I spearheaded the restyling of Valentino's intranet portal, a vital platform utilized by Valentino employees. Key functionalities encompassed calendar management, company news dissemination, social and magazine streams, and access to online courses.

    • 2020

  3. Main Website

    IED

    I contributed to the restyling of the main website for IED, a renowned design institute with locations in Italy, Spain, Brazil, and China, serving over 100,000 students. The updated site is scheduled for publication in 2021.

    • Web
    • 2020

  4. Data Visualization

    • 2018
